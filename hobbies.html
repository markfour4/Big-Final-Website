When I have free time, this is what I like to do:

I watch a lot of Youtube videos when I have the chance to. My favourite channel to watch is Lemmino, he does video documentaries in great detail, which are very entertaining. I would definitely recommend!

I like playing my Switch becuase you can play it wherever you are. I use my Switch to play games, such as Pokemon and Mario Kart. Both games are great fun, and I would highly recommend them.

I listen to music in my free time. I don't have a specific genre I enjoy, but I mostly listen to music from the 80's, such as "Somebody's Watching Me" by Rockwell,"C-Moon" by Paul McCartney, & "Smooth Criminal" by Michael Jackson.

I walk with my dog every night before bed, and sometimes we go on an entire adventure around my estate. A walk before bed is good for your health, and ensures that the dog doesn't leave a nasty surprise in the morning!

Minecraft is my favourite game of all time. I like making adventure worlds for other people to play on. There's so much to do in this game, you could make an entire society about it and you wouldn't even scratch the surface!

I can play the piano at a beginner level. However, I like to learn music by ear and then play it on the piano. I find it more fun to learn it myself!

I like drawing in my free time! Whether it is on paper or digitally, I find it relaxing. Fun fact: I made all of these icons on this page! It was very fun to make them.

Bicycle! Bicycle! Bicycle! I enjoy riding my bike every now and then when the weather's nice. I cycle in the park in my area, and it's not only great fun, but it's also healthy!
